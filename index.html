Severity glossary
High
Needs action now: any asset in Overdue Return status, an ex-TM still holding an In Use asset, In Use with no assignee, or a Pending Return held by a separated TM for 31+ days (or with no separation date on file).
Medium
Investigate: ex-TM with an asset in any transitional status, a transitional status stale for 91+ days, or Pending Return held by a separated TM within 30 days of separation.
Low
Monitor: transitional status stale 31 to 90 days. Transitional = Defective, Under Repair, For Inspection, In Configuration, Pending Deployment, Pending Disposal, Pending Sale, Pending Return.
Clean
No flags: record is healthy, in a final state (Sold/Disposed/Decommissioned), or transitional for 30 days or less.
